About Me

It seems that I've done a little bit of everything in the tech industry. I got my start as an embedded systems engineer writing software in C and various assembly languages. After a few years of "bit-bashing" I transitioned to academia and worked several years teaching computer science. In the last few years I've been doing mostly front-end development, where I've mostly codied in HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

Currently I'm focused on transitioning my career to UX Design. I really like that UX melds my experience in creative problem solving with my empathetic focus on customers/users. I'm currently working on a few UX projects and will be adding more to my UX portfolio.
